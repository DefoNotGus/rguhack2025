Game Instructions

You are a student in the RGU chemistry lab. Suddenly, a fire breaks out in the building. Your mission is to escape alive, but it won't be easy. The fire is spreading quickly, and toxic smoke fills the hallways. You'll need to make quick decisions and use the right protective equipment to survive.
